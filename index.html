What is a “working day”

A workday is defined as your FTE × 8 hours.

When does my HL expire?

You must use all HL hours by the end of the fiscal year on 6/30 (waiting until the last minute may create complications with your pay).

What happens if my HL expires?

You lose the ability to use it as leave time but it will be paid out in the fall (Sept/Oct) it will appear as “Holiday Pay Out - Non-Ex” on your paystub.

When can VL be used?

VL can be used after it has been accrued and approved outside of the Winter Leave time Blackout period.

When does my VL expire?

VL expires on September 30th (see your leave balance in My UK for expiring hour totals).

What happens if I call in sick?

If you have enough SL available you will be paid the hours scheduled in SL hours for regular shifts, overtime shifts will not be compensated with SL.
If you do not have enough SL to cover your required minimum hours for the shift then we use SL>HL(available for use)>VL in this order to get you to the required hours.
If you have exhausted all leave time you will receive “no pay” hours for the deficit
Taking a “no pay” instead of using your leave balances if available is not an option.

What happens if I am WEPP and I call in for a shift?

Since WEPPs do not accrue leave time outside of VL all call-ins will be paid from VL it will appear as “WEPP SL” on your timecard.

Can WEPPs take a Well-being Day?

Since WEPPS do not accrue SL they are unable to take a Well-being Day.

What if I am a WEPP that transferred in with a SL balance?

Even with a previous SL balance the SL is unavailable to use when working in the WEPP role.

Can I take FL as a WEPP?

FL will be excused but to be paid you must have a balance in the VL as it is the only leave you accrue.

How can I take a Well-being day?

Must be prescheduled.
You can not call in and use a WBD to cover the shift.
You have to have enough SL to use WBD as it pulls from your SL balance.
You are entitled to 2 WBD per fiscal year this is based on your normal working hours

How do I use Family Medical Leave?

Continuous FML is entered by the FML coordinators office and the timeframe it applies to is based on the provided approval letter.
Intermittent FML is applied on an as-needed basis and is done through the normal call-in procedure
